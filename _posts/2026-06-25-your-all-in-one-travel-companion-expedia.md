## What is Expedia?

Expedia is one of the world's leading online travel agencies, offering a comprehensive platform for booking flights, hotels, car rentals, cruise ships, and vacation packages[reference:0]. Launched in 1996 as a small startup inside Microsoft, Expedia has evolved across three major technology waves—from the dawn of the internet through the mobile shift and now into the AI era[reference:1]. Today, it operates one of the world's most trusted and intelligent travel marketplaces, connecting millions of travelers with partners across lodging, air, car, and activities[reference:2].

Expedia Group's portfolio includes over 200 travel booking websites, including Hotels.com, Vrbo, Orbitz, Trivago, and Travelocity, serving 75 countries in 35 languages with over 600 million monthly visits[reference:3]. Its flagship **One Key** loyalty program unifies rewards across Expedia, Hotels.com, and Vrbo, creating a seamless experience for travelers[reference:4].
