## What is Tripadvisor?

Tripadvisor is the world's largest travel guidance platform[reference:0]. With over **one billion reviews and contributions** from travelers worldwide, it helps millions of people discover, plan, and book the perfect trip[reference:1][reference:2]. Whether you're looking for hotels, restaurants, flights, or experiences, Tripadvisor brings together trusted guidance from real travelers to help you make confident decisions.

## Why Travelers Choose Tripadvisor

### 1. Real Reviews from Real Travelers

Tripadvisor is built on the power of community. With over one billion reviews and contributions[reference:3], you get honest, unfiltered feedback from travelers just like you[reference:4]. Access millions of reviews about hotels, restaurants, tours, attractions, and other experiences—all from people who have been there[reference:5].

### 2. AI-Powered Trip Planning

Tripadvisor is leading the travel industry's AI revolution with innovative tools that make trip planning faster, smarter, and more personalized[reference:6].

- **AI Trip Planner**: A generative AI-powered tool that helps travelers build personalized itineraries through conversational prompts[reference:7]. Simply ask questions like "What are the best things to do in Lisbon this weekend with kids?" and get tailored recommendations instantly[reference:8].

- **ChatGPT Integration**: Tripadvisor has launched an app on ChatGPT that enables users to access reviews, ratings, and images directly within their chat[reference:9]. The goal is to make trip planning highly personalized by combining all Tripadvisor content with OpenAI's advanced AI capabilities[reference:10].

- **Perplexity Partnership**: In January 2025, Tripadvisor partnered with Perplexity to integrate its billion reviews and Viator's 300,000+ experiences with AI-powered search[reference:11]. This collaboration delivers personalized, actionable recommendations that simplify the trip-planning process[reference:12].

Travelers who use Tripadvisor's generative AI tools spend **2 to 3 times more** than those using traditional interfaces[reference:13]. The AI not only simplifies planning but also surfaces better, more relevant options[reference:14].
